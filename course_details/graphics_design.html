Graphics Designing

Crafting Creativity, Shaping Perspectives: Explore the World of Graphic Design

Objectives

Develop fundamental design skills, including typography, color theory, and layout composition. Cultivate proficiency in industry-standard design software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. Foster creativity and problem-solving abilities through hands-on projects and real-world design challenges. Understand the principles of branding, visual communication, and user experience design. Equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to produce professional-quality graphic designs across various mediums and platforms.
