Web Development (Full Stack)

Unlock the Full Potential of Web Development

Objectives

The objective of the Full Stack Web Development course is to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of both front-end and back-end web technologies, frameworks, and best practices. Through hands-on projects and theoretical learning, students will gain proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and associated libraries and frameworks for building dynamic and responsive user interfaces. Additionally, they will delve into server-side programming languages like Node.js, Python, or Ruby, along with databases such as SQL or NoSQL, to develop scalable and secure web applications. By mastering the full stack of web development, students will be prepared to create robust, modern web solutions that meet industry standards and demands.
